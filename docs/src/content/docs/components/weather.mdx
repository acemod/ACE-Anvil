---
title: Weather
---

Adds a toggleable display for showing information about wind direction and strength.

## Usage

### Show Wind Info

- Toggle wind info display with `Show wind info` keybind in `ACE` category (Default: <kbd>Shift</kbd>+<kbd>K</kbd>).
- The wind direction and strength are indicated by a [wind barb](https://www.weather.gov/hfo/windbarbinfo) in the top left corner.
